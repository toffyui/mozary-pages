Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

Mozary does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. All data is processed and stored locally on your device. The App does not use any analytics, tracking, or advertising services.

Overview

Mozary ("the App") is a mosaic art creation app. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This policy explains how the App handles your data.

Data Collection

Mozary does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. All data is processed and stored locally on your device. The App does not use any analytics, tracking, or advertising services.

Photo Library Access

The App requests access to your photo library to select images for mosaic art creation and to save exported works. Your photos are never uploaded to any server. Thumbnails are cached locally on your device for performance purposes only.

In-App Purchases

In-app purchases are processed entirely through Apple's StoreKit framework. The App does not have access to your payment information. Purchase records are managed by Apple and associated with your Apple ID.

Data Storage

Project data (mosaic configurations, thumbnails) and app settings are stored locally on your device. This data is not synced to any cloud service. Deleting the App will remove all locally stored data.

Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or data collection services. The only external communication is with Apple's servers for processing in-app purchases.

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any personal information from children. Since no data is collected from any user, the App is safe for use by all ages.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the App with an updated revision date.

Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at mozary.app@gmail.com.
